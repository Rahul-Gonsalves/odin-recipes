Orange Creamsicle Protein Shake

The recipe and image is taken from allrecipes

This short and simple recipe will provide one serving of a delicious protein shake in just 5 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 orange
  • 5.3 ounces of greek yogurt
  • 5 icecubes
  • 1 scoop of your favorite vanilla protein powder
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Cut the orange into segments (keep some zest)
  2. poor all ingredients into a blender
  3. Blend until smooth and enjoy!
