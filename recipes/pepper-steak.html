Slow-Cooker Pepper Steak

The recipe and image is taken from allrecipes

This pepper steak recipe will produce some of the most flavorful and tender food.
It's great to serve over rice or noodles

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef sirloon, cut into 2 inch strips
  • 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cube beef bouillon
  • 1/4 cupe hot water
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 large green bell peppers, roughly chopped
  • 1 (14.5) ounce can stewed tomatoes, with liquid
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Sprinkle beef with garlic powder and heat with all vegetable oil over medium heat 5 minutes per side
  3. transfre meet to a low cooker
  4. In a different container, mix bouillon cube with hot water until dissolved, then mix in cornstarch till dissolved
  5. Pour into the slow cooker with beef, stir in onion, green peppers, tomatoes, soy sauce, sugar, and salt.
  6. Cover and cook on High for 3-4 hours or low for 6-8 hours
