Slow Cooker Texas Smoked Beef Brisket

The recipe and image is taken from allrecipes

Easy brisket recipe in a crockpot that allows for a great meal on busy days, letting it cook without much micromanaging
This will give about 4 servings

Ingredients

Brisket Rub:

  • 3 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 2 tablespoons ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 lbs beef brisket

Barbeque Sauce:

  • 3/4 cup Barbeque sauce
  • 1/4 cup water (optional)
  • 1 tablespoons worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon liquid smoke flavoring
  • 1/2 onion, sliced into rings

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Prepare brisket rub: mix all ingredients for it
  3. Rub evenly over brisket, put it in a resealable plastic bag, refrigerate for 30 minutes-overnight
  4. Prepare bbq sauce, stir all ingredients for it in the bottom of a slow cooker
  5. Remove brisket from the refrigerato and take it out of the bag. Lay it in the slow cooker
  6. Arrage onions over the brisket
  7. Cook on low until brisket is tender, 6-7 hours
  8. Rest the brisket for 10 minutes before slicing
  9. Serve with the sauce from the slow cooker
